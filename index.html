UM Applied Statistics Reading Group

Welcome!

We’re a group of graduate students in the Department of Statistics at the University of Michigan interested in applications of statistics to problems of societal interest. Our reading group is meant to be a relaxed environment for discussion, clarification, and inquiry into the many challenges of deploying statistics in the real world. ___
